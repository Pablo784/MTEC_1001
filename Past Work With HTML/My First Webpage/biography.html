Biography Page



Post Malone or Austin Richard Post who was born on July 4,1995 in Syracuse, New York and was raised by his father Rich Post, and his stepmother Jodie. His father was a DJ during his youth which gave Post a chance to expose him to different genres of music and that is the basis for his future career in music. Post is a American rapper, singer, songwriter and record producer. In 2015, he realeased his debut single "White Iverson." Afterwards, he signed a recording contract with Republic Records. His net-worth is currently $24 Million and he's been active since 2013-present. His Debut album Stoney from 2016 which features his hit single "Congratulations and his second album beerbongs and bentleys from 2018 which features the Billboard Hot 100 number-one singles "Rockstar" and "Psycho" which on that same year it was nominated as Album of the year at the 2018 Grammys.
